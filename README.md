## Overview
This repository contains the foundational principles of the **Algorithm of 11 Properties** — a strategic framework designed to transform the primary impulse of Will into tangible, material Realization.

## The Core Formula
The efficiency of any realization can be expressed through the lens of resistance:

$$S = \frac{C}{M \cdot A}$$

Where:
* **S** = Result (Success/Materialization)
* **C** = Consciousness/Intent
* **M** * **A** = Resistance of the Environment (Mass * Acceleration of opposing forces)

## The 11 Properties of the Algorithm

1. **Will (The Primary Impulse)** — Subjectivity and intent. The "Start" button of the universe.
2. **Wisdom** — Pattern recognition, intuition, and the system's memory.
3. **Knowledge** — Facts, hard invariants, and physical laws.
4. **Comprehension (The Pause)** — Synchronization of the internal and external. The "Aha!" moment.
5. **Projective Freedom** — Imagination, the blueprint of the future, a realm without boundaries.
6. **Projective Constraint** — Ergonomics, body parameters, and technical frameworks.
7. **Balance (0.618)** — The Golden Ratio. Dynamic equilibrium in every decision.
8. **Practical Freedom** — Iterations, the right to make mistakes, and exploring options.
9. **Practical Constraint** — Resistance of the environment ($M \cdot A$), the density of reality, and deadlines.
10. **Support (The Foundation)** — Security, the structural frame, and the ground you stand on.
11. **Realization** — Finalization. The transformation of spirit into matter ($S$).
